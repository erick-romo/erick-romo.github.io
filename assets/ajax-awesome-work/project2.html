Legal Intake Form
Erick Chavarria
This was a mock-up project for a network of legal organizations in the San Francisco Bay Area that needed a system to track potential clients. The website takes up all the information from someone and is then sent, via an email template, to an organization that has the capacity and resources to serve the person.
